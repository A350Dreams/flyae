About Air Europa Virtual

Air Europa Virtual (AEV) is engineered for the discerning flight simulator enthusiast. We bridge the gap between virtual flying and real-world airline operations, offering an intricate system that mirrors Air Europa's routes, fleet, and operational protocols.

Our dynamic environment encourages pilots to push their boundaries, engage in challenging scenarios, and master complex aircraft systems. AEV is a community built on shared passion, where collaboration and continuous learning are at the core of every flight.

Experience advanced flight tracking, participate in global events, and become part of a forward-thinking virtual airline—all completely free of charge.

Join Our Dynamic Discord Hub

The AEV Discord server is more than just a chatroom; it's our command center. Here, you'll find real-time support, active discussions on aviation, and the opportunity to connect directly with fellow pilots and staff.

  • Live Flight Planning: Collaborate on routes, weather, and NOTAMs with experienced virtual pilots.
  • Technical Support: Get assistance with simulator setups, addon configurations, and flight tracking software.
  • Exclusive Events: Participate in scheduled group flights, cross-network events, and special challenges.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Access a rich repository of operational documents, flight guides, and tutorials.
  • Community Engagement: Share your flight experiences, screenshots, and videos, fostering a vibrant, supportive atmosphere.
