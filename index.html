About Air Europa Virtual

Air Europa Virtual (AEV) is not just a community; it's a commitment to realistic flight simulation. We provide a platform for virtual pilots to engage with the intricate details of aviation, from flight planning to procedural execution, all inspired by Air Europa's operational standards.

Our dedication to realism is matched by our supportive environment. AEV fosters a community where pilots of all experience levels can refine their skills, share their passion, and embark on challenging flights within a meticulously structured virtual airline framework.

Discover a world where your virtual flights truly matter, contribute to a professional virtual airline, and never pay a fee to join this exceptional experience.

Our Exclusive Discord Community

The AEV Discord server is the nexus of our professional community. It's an active hub where pilots collaborate, share advanced techniques, and receive real-time support. This is where your virtual aviation journey truly takes flight.

  • Expert Support: Direct access to experienced staff and fellow pilots for simulator configuration, flight planning, and procedural guidance.
  • Dynamic Events: Participate in meticulously planned group flights, realistic online ATC sessions, and engaging challenges designed to test and improve your skills.
  • Knowledge Base: Access a curated library of resources, including SIDs/STARs, real-world operational procedures, and exclusive training materials.
  • Career Progression: Discuss and track your virtual pilot career, exchange tips on maximizing realism, and share your achievements.
  • Networking: Connect with a global network of dedicated flight simulation enthusiasts.

Join our vibrant and highly professional Discord community to elevate your flight simulation experience.
