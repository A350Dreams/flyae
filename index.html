About Air Europa Virtual

Air Europa Virtual (AEV) is a thriving community dedicated to the art of flight simulation, mirroring the real-world operations of Air Europa. Our mission is to provide an immersive and authentic experience for virtual pilots worldwide.

We welcome pilots of all skill levels to our friendly and supportive environment. Whether you're mastering short-haul European routes or embarking on challenging intercontinental journeys, AEV offers a dynamic platform to enhance your virtual aviation passion.

Join us to engage in realistic flight planning, participate in exciting community events, and contribute to a vibrant virtual airline, all without any membership fees.

Connect on Discord

Our Discord server is the pulsing heart of the AEV community. It's the go-to place for pilots to connect, share insights, seek support, and participate in all our virtual airline activities.

  • Live Chat: Engage with fellow pilots in real-time.
  • Flight Resources: Access charts, guides, and planning tools.
  • Events & Activities: Join group flights, challenges, and training.
